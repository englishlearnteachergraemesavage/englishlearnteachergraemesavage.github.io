Teacher English

Hello, my name is Graeme. I’ve been teaching online for about two and a half years and I love it. My lessons usually focus on conversation practice with feedback and homework for common grammatical mistakes. I also help students prepare for professional interviews, improve vocabulary and pronunciation, write work emails, and edit school work. I enjoy teaching online and admire learners who live and work using a second language. I volunteered with The Intercultural Association of Victoria helping immigrants practice their English. This inspired me to make teaching a bigger part of my life. As a result, I pursued a 120-hour certification and a 30-hour online teaching certificate. I am trying to learn Mandarin Chinese, Farsi, and Spanish so I understand how hard learning a new language can be.

I have a 120-hour English teaching certification and a 30-hour online teaching certificate. I am always improving my methods and strategies for teaching.

I am learning Mandarin Chinese, Persian, and Spanish online, so I know what it is like to be a student like you. I keep in mind the difficulties of learning a language online when I teach. I always try to provide a mix of activities practicing reading, writing, listening, and most importantly, speaking.

I moved to South America in November 2021 and since then I have been learning Spanish through immersion. I am inspired by my students to learn a second language!

I can help you with pronunciation, preparing for job interviews, professional English, work emails, conversation practice, and much more. During my lessons, I aim to increase your confidence in using English and help you achieve your goals. Send me a message or book a lesson today. I look forward to meeting you!
